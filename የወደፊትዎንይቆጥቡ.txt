I did not stay, yet they asked questions.

I simply loved you, while you were searching for something more; yet, apart from my death, I had no answers about your future.

Remain, while you were going to the extreme.

So turn to me — without me being your worst hell — and save your future; yet this is all I can offer: live your life with me.

Even in the darkest times, tomorrow is always the best.

Because you were there, I was not alone that day.

I do not remember the end of that tomorrow.

I remember your smile.

I remember your laughter.

I remember your hope.

I remember everything, all day long.

I remember what would have happened if tomorrow had never ended — I love you.
